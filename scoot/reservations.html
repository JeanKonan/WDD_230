Rental Agreement Information

Please review and agree to the following terms and conditions before making a reservation:

  • Security Deposit: A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations.
  • Driver's License: A valid driver's license is required at the time of rental.
  • Age Restriction: No one under the age of 12 years is allowed to ride scooters.
  • Fuel Charge: Fuel charge is not included in the rental prices.
  • Unlimited Mileage: Enjoy unlimited mileage during your rental.
  • Liability Insurance: Liability insurance is included in the rental.
  • Damage Insurance: Optional damage insurance is available for purchase.
  • Free Delivery: Free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs for 2+ day rentals.
  • Payment Options: Payment accepted via Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Venmo.
  • Return Locations: Rentals may be returned to either physical location.
